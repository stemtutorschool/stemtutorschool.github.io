Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which class is right for my child?

We assess each student individually to determine the best placement. Our assessment evaluates their current mathematical understanding and problem-solving abilities, not just grade level.

When can my child start classes?

We typically start new cohorts at the beginning of fall, winter, and spring terms. However, we may be able to accommodate mid-term enrollment depending on class availability and the student's level.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer online class. Our online classes follow the same curriculum and maintain the same level of interactive learning and individual attention.
